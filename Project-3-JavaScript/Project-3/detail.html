This shoe puts a fresh spin on what you know best: crisp leather, bold colors and the perfect amount of flash to make you shine. A subtle platform gives you just the right amount of height.


Benefits

  • Smooth leather with stitched overlays ages to soft perfection.
  • Originally designed for hoops, Nike Air cushioning adds lightweight, all-day comfort.
  • Padded, low-cut collar looks sleek and feels great.

Product Details

  • Foam midsole
  • Rubber outsole
  • Perforations on the toe

Shoe Origin

Debuting in 1982, the AF1 was the first basketball shoe to house Nike Air, revolutionizing the game while rapidly gaining traction around the world. Today, the Air Force 1 stays true to its roots with the same soft and springy cushioning that changed sneaker history.
